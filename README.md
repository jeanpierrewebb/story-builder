## The Problem

College admissions consultants charge $3,000-$50,000 to help students find their "narrative." The core of what they do:
1. Deep reflection exercises
2. Pattern recognition across experiences
3. Theme identification
4. Positioning for essays

**Story Builder democratizes this process.**

## Philosophy

We believe in **scaffolding, not writing**. Story Builder helps you discover and articulate your story ‚Äî it never writes for you. That would be:
1. Unethical
2. Detectable
3. Counterproductive

Your voice is your greatest asset. We just help you find it.
